Merrill Moat Day

Saturday, April 27, 11 a.m.-2 p.m., Merrill Moat

 

Buy and tie-dye a Merrill t-shirt and check out the new Moat paintings in progress. Talk to current Merrill student leaders and find out what students are up to at Merrill in 2013. Also be sure to check out the Merrill Renovation relief map and drawings of our renovation plans!
