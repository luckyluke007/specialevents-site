2011 Commencement Schedule

Commencement exercises for the Class of 2011 at the University of California, Santa Cruz, will be held on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday, June 10-12. Commencements will be hosted by UCSC's 10 colleges and the Graduate Division. A number of less formal celebrations, beginning June 9, are also planned to honor graduates.

The campus press release contains information on number of students expected to earn degrees.

A web feature also profiles 10 extraordinary members of the Class of 2011. See our 10 Grads in 10 Days feature.
