Commencement 2012 slideshow

Commencement exercises at the University of California, Santa Cruz, were held Friday, Saturday, and Sunday, June 15-17. A total of 4,248 UC Santa Cruz students graduated during the 2011-2012 academic year, including 2,257 candidates this spring for bachelor of arts, music, or science degrees.
