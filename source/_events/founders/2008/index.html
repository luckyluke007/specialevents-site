Founders Day 2008

Three outstanding individuals were honored by UC Santa Cruz at the annual Founders Day dinner, on Friday, October 24, 2008. Honorees were Narinder Singh Kapany, Dana Priest, and Patricia Zavella. For more information, please read about this year's honorees.

Missed the inaugural Founders Day Dinner? Please see the 2007 Founders Day web site, which includes video tributes to last year's honorees.

Fulfilling the Promise

The second annual UC Santa Cruz Founders Day gala dinner drew more than 350 people to the Cocoanut Grove on October 24th to celebrate the founding ideals of the campus and the outstanding accomplishments of three individuals with ties to UCSC. [More]
