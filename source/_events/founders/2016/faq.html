FAQ

Q: Where is the Science and Engineering Library?  

A: The library is located on Science Hill click here

Q: What is the dress code? 

A: The dress code is “festive cocktail attire.” Please be aware that guests will walk a short distance (.2 mile) from the Core West parking garage to the main entrance of the Science and Engineering Library. 

Q: How long will the evening be?

A: The reception begins at 6 p.m. outdoors on the plaza in front of the library; dinner starts at 7 p.m. The dinner and program will end approximately 9:00 p.m.

Q: What is the seating format? Is it assigned? Will I be able to sit with my friends/family? 

A: Yes, seating will be assigned. The Special Events Office will seat you together with your guests and will work to accommodate any seating requests that were noted in your registration form. Please contact us if you need to make changes.

Q: What is the parking situation? 

A: There will be complimentary parking in the Core West parking garage. Guests in wheelchairs who need a wheelchair lift, or those who are are unable to step up into the disability van, will be directed by parking staff. 

Q: Will there be a coat check? 

A: Yes.

Q: Will there be photos after the event?

A: Yes. The UC Santa Cruz University Relations office will send guests a follow-up with a recap, photos, and more. 

Additional questions please call the Special Events Office (831) 459-5003 or specialevents@ucsc.edu
