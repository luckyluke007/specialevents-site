Founders Day 2009

Five outstanding individuals were honored by UC Santa Cruz at the annual Founders Day dinner on Friday, October 23, 2009. They are Ed Catmull, Patricia (Porter '88) and Rowland Rebele, John R. Rickford (Stevenson ’71), and Dan Friedman. Read more about this year's honorees.

Fulfilling the Promise

UCSC's third annual Founders Day event celebrated the spirit of community and honored five individuals for outstanding achievement. Ed Catmull was presented with the Foundation Medal, Patricia (Porter '88) and Rowland Rebele with the Fiat Lux Award, and John R. Rickford (Stevenson ’71) with the Alumni Achievement Award. In addition, Dan Friedman was honored for his selection as this year's UCSC Faculty Research Lecturer. [More]
