Sponsorship Opportunities

MATCH YOUR GIFT

Two alumni donors and Hierarchical Systems Research Foundation have provided a matching challenge to encourage first-time and increased donations. Qualifying funds include Regents Scholars, the Student Assistance Emergency Fund, and scholarships supporting students majoring in the physical and biological sciences or engineering. Increased giving beyond last year’s donation will be tripled. New gifts will be doubled.

Questions: please e-mail sbd@ucsc.edu

Sponsorship — Levels & Benefits

$1,000 — GUEST OF MENTION

Two complimentary seats at the dinner.

Recognition in the Scholarship Benefit Dinner program.

Recognition in post-event advertisements.

($500 to dinner/$500 to scholarships)

$2,500 — SUPPORTER

Four complimentary seats at dinner. 

Increased recognition in the Scholarship Benefit Dinner program.

Increased recognition on the Scholarship Benefit Dinner web site.

Increased recognition in postevent advertisements

$5,000 — FRIEND

Equivalent to 1/2 the annual registration fees, plus books and supplies for one student, or equivalent to covering books and supplies, transportation costs, and personal expenses for a full year.

Complimentary table for ten, with table signage. 

Increased recognition in the Scholarship Benefit Dinner program.

Increased recognition on the Scholarship Benefit Dinner web site.

Name featured in event promotions and advertisements.

Name featured prominently on event invitation (sponsorships received by January 4, 2013).

Opportunity to meet Chancellor George Blumenthal and other dignitaries.

$10,000 — SUPPORTING UNDERWRITER

Equivalent to annual registration fees, plus books and supplies for one student.

Benefits previously listed plus:

Verbal recognition from Chancellor George Blumenthal during the event.

Name featured on large screen during program.

$15,000— LEAD UNDERWRITER

Equivalent to annual registration fees, books, supplies and personal expenses for one student for a full year.

Benefits previously listed plus:

Prominent mention in postevent article in UCSC E-newsletter sent to over 100,000 alumni, parents, staff and faculty, students,and friends.

Prominent recognition in the periodic report of the UC Santa Cruz Foundation.

Opportunity to provide a special favor or keepsake for event attendees.

$25,000 — PRESENTING UNDERWRITER

Equivalent to annual registration fees, books and supplies, transportation, personal expenses for one student.

Benefits previously listed plus:

Special recognition by Chancellor Blumenthal during the event. Recognition luncheon with graduate and undergraduate scholarship recipients.
