Cowell Coffee in the Courtyard

Saturday, April 27, 8:30-10 a.m., Cowell Courtyard

Start your morning by enjoying the stunning view of Monterey Bay over a delicious brunch in the company of friends old and new.

Cowell Alumni Discussion: Entertaining Ideas

Saturday, April 27, 10-11:30 a.m., Page Smith Library

After brunch, we will move into the Page Smith Library at 10:00 a.m. for the Cowell Colloquium titled "Entertaining Ideas." This event will feature Mary-Kay Gamel, Professor of Classical Studies and Feminist Studies; balloon artist Addi Somekh (Cowell '94); and Paul Rabwin (Cowell '70), producer of The X-Files. These wonderful speakers will discuss different facets of the arts in their lives, their talents, and their specialties.

Throughout the weekend, view these exhibits:

• Bonnie Stone, watercolor artist whose work celebrates the lives of women of multiple diasporas, Cowell Eloise Pickard Smith Gallery

• Dizikes art cases across from the Fireside Lounge
