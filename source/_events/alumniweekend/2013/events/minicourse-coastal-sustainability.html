Minicourse in Coastal Sustainability with Gary Griggs

Saturday, April 27, 1-4 p.m., Long Marine Lab

Find out what we’re learning about coastal sustainability and where our research is likely to go in the future. You’ll join Gary Griggs in celebrating the 35th anniversary of Long Marine Lab! As director of the Institute of Marine Sciences, Professor Griggs—who is celebrating 45 years of teaching at UCSC—will share the lab’s history. You’ll hear from research partners, such as the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Association (NOAA) and the California Department of Fish and Wildlife. See our marine mammal facilities, including dolphins, Puka and Primo, and meet a student docent from the Seymour Marine Discovery Center to learn about UCSC’s public outreach.
