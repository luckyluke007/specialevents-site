Oakes Celebration of Don Rothman's Life and Achievements

Saturday, April 27, 10-11:30 a.m., Oakes 106

Don Rothman joined the faculty at Oakes College in 1973. A leading voice for writers and writing, he touched the lives of many students, faculty, and staff over his 39-year career at UCSC. Please join us in remembering and celebrating his legacy. 

David Anthony, former Oakes Provost, and David Dodson, former Oakes College Administrative Officer, will facilitate a memorial celebration in honor of Don Rothman's life, including the unveiling and dedication of a memorial plaque in his honor.
