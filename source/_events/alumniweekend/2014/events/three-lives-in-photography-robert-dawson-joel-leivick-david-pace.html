Three Lives in Photography: Robert Dawson, Joel Leivick, David Pace

Saturday, April 26,  Mary Porter Sesnon Art Gallery

Reception, 4 – 6 p.m.

Gallery walk-though with the artists, 5 – 6 p.m.

Join fellow art lovers for a reception and gallery walk-through with three UC Santa Cruz alumni photographers who have dedicated their lives to capturing images and sharing their vision of the world.

Robert Dawson (Merrill, '72, psychology) has examined water in the West and other global water issues in a series of exhibitions and book projects.
Joel Leivick (Porter, '72, aesthetic studies, individual major) makes landscapes that explore and exalt their subjects with great lyricism. He has been teaching at Stanford University for the past 32 years.
David Pace (Stevenson, '74, individual major) has been building an extensive body of work that focuses on the small West African country of Burkina Faso. He has many stories to share., with

This exhibition traces three UCSC alumni photographers in their careers as educators, environmentalists, and photographers. The Mary Porter Sesnon Gallery will be hosting this exhibit from April 9 though May 10. The gallery will be open to vistors all day on Saturday, April 26 from 12 – 6 p.m.

This special Alumni Weekend event will be held in conjunction with the Porter Alumni Wine Reception on Saturday, April 26.  The reception will take place from 4 – 6 p.m. and the gallery walk-though will take place from 5 – 6 p.m. 

For any further questions, please contact Sesnon Gallery Director and Curator Shelby Graham.
