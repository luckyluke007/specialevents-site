Tour of the UCSC Farm

Saturday, April 26, 10 – 11:15 a.m.

UCSC Farm - Meet at the Louise Cain Gatehouse

Join us for a guided tour of the 30-acre UC Santa Cruz Farm, which is managed by the Center for Agroecology and Sustainable Food Systems (CASFS).

Founded in 1971, the UCSC Farm offers vibrant examples of organic agriculture and horticulture, and gives students and community members a place to learn about organic gardening and farming techniques as well as larger issues facing our food system. We look forward to sharing the latest information on education, research, and outreach taking place at the Farm and at the Alan Chadwick Garden through CASFS.

Please wear comfortable walking shoes. Parking is available at the Carriage House lot (lot 116, Coolidge Drive near the Carriage House). Meet at the Louise Cain Gatehouse. Click here for a map and directions.

Check our website and Facebook page to learn more about the UCSC Farm. For more information, please contact CASFS Outreach and Recruitment Coordinator Melissa Betrone.
