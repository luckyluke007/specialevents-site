SOMeCA 25th Anniversary

Saturday, April 26, 6 – 9 p.m.

Cowell Dining Hall

Register for Alumni Weekend using the blue button above. 

Click here to RSVP for the SOMeCA 25th Anniversary event.

SOAR/Student Media/Cultural Arts and Diversity (SOMeCA) invites current student organization leadership to a special dinner discussion with former student organizers and current community leaders. This year’s event will celebrate 25 years of SOAR at UC Santa Cruz.

The panel of speakers was selected by a committee of current student organizers and will be made up of alumni who are leaders in their fields. We look forward to seeing everyone again. Please click here to learn more about what is going on at SOAR today.

For more information, please contact SOAR at (831) 459-2934.
