Natural History Field Quarter Happy Hour Celebration

Saturday, April 26, 5 – 7 p.m. 

Rosie McCann's Irish Pub, 1220 Pacific Ave, Santa Cruz

Come raise a glass with Field Quarter alumni and staff at Rosie McCann's Irish Pub in downtown Santa Cruz. We had so much fun at the reunion two years ago we want to get together again!  After two years of hard work since the NHFQ reunion in 2012, our fundraising efforts have been wildly successful! Join us for the party and we'll fill you in on all the details. We have a lot to celebrate! Long live Field Quarter! 

Join alums and staff of the Natural History Field Quarter this Alumni Weekend and help us inaugurate a new group, The Friends of the Norris Natural History Programs. We'll be celebrating exciting news around the future of Field Quarter, the UCSC Natural History Museum and the Environmental Field Program. Share your ideas, toast the future and reminisce with great folks at Rosie McCann's Irish pub. We'll provide some appetizers and there will be a no host bar.

Check out the NHFQ Facebook Page for event updates to get in touch with other Field Quarter Alums.

For more information, please contact Erika Perloff.
