UCSC Alumni Association Council Ballot

Reunion Weekend is just one of the important programs supported by your UCSC Alumni Association membership dollars, thank you for making this weekend possible! The UCSC Alumni Association annual meeting will take place during Alumni Reunion Weekend at the Humanities Lecture Hall on Saturday, April 28, 2012 at 2:30 p.m. in conjunction with the Academic Forum.

Your UCSC Alumni Association membership entitles you to be a part of the alumni voice on campus. Vote today for your fellow alumni who represent you on the UCSC Alumni Association Council.  Click Here to download your ballot (pdf). 

We hope you are able to return to campus and join your fellow alumni to reminisce, revisit and rekindle memories from the good old days as a banana slug.
