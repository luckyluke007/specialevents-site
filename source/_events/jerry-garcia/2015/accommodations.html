Accommodations

  • Dream Inn

    • 175 West Cliff Drive
    • Santa Cruz, CA 95060
    • Phone: 831-460-5007
    • Map

      Located on Cowell's beach, adjacent to the wharf and historic beach boardwalk, Dream Inn is ideal for exploring Santa Cruz and has views of Monterey Bay from every room.

      A special UCSC room rate has been arranged at the Dream Inn.  Quantities are limited, good for only  the night of 8.1.15,and must be reserved by 7.1.15 to qualify.  You may take advantage of this discount by entering 1508UCSCJE in the "Group ID" field when you register online, or by calling the hotel directly  at 831 460-5007 and asking for this discount.

    • The Hilton

      • 6001 La Madrona Drive
      • Santa Cruz, CA 95060
      • Phone: 831-440-1000
      • Map 

      This hotel is located just 10 minutes from downtown Santa Cruz, and offers a full range of amenities.

      Make reservations at the Hilton Santa Cruz - Scotts Valley or call 831-440-1000

    • Hotel Paradox

      • 611 Ocean Street
      • Santa Cruz, CA 95060
      • Phone: 831-425-7100
      • Map | Reservations

      The new Hotel Paradox in beautiful Santa Cruz, California, is introducing a chic vibe to hotel lodging on the Central Coast. Designed with a fine eye to detail, the boutique Hotel Paradox features top-level hospitality, sophisticated hotel rooms and suites, modern amenities, meeting space, and a full-service restaurant and bar. Experience Santa Cruz from the new perspective of Hotel Paradox.

      Make reservations at the Hotel Paradox or call 831-425-7100
